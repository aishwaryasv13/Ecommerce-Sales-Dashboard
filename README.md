# Ecommerce-Sales-Dashboard
Interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard built with Power BI — tracks YTD Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Margin with regional breakdowns, product performance, and shipping analysis.


E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing e-commerce performance across customer segments, product categories, and U.S. regions.

Key Features:
KPI Cards — YTD Sales ($11.53M), Profit ($1.34M), Quantity (107.2K), and Profit Margin (11.58%) with YoY trend indicators
Sales by Category — Year-over-year comparison across Technology, Office Supplies, and Furniture
Top & Bottom 5 Products — Identifies best and worst performing SKUs by YTD revenue
Geographic Map — State-level sales distribution across West, East, Central, and South regions
Donut Charts — Regional share and shipping type breakdown (Standard, Second, First Class, Same Day)
Segment Filter — Slicers for Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments

Tools & Skills:
Power BI | DAX Data | Visualization | Sales Analytics | KPI | Reporting
